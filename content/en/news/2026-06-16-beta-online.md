The **National Digital Sovereignty Observatory** is online in **beta**: both the **initiative's website** (this one) and the **technical software** that produces the data, **[MxMap.it](https://mxmap.it/)**.

## What is online

- **The Observatory website** — reports, analysis by category of body, sections dedicated to decision-makers, stakeholders, journalists, researchers and citizens, downloadable documents and open data.
- **The MxMap software** — the open source pipeline that analyses the DNS records of the Italian PA domains to identify the email provider and its jurisdiction.

The published data is **preliminary**: the first official measurement, from which continuous monitoring will start, is still being consolidated. This is why the project is marked as **beta**.

## An invitation to take part and contribute

The Observatory is an **open, multi-stakeholder** project. Everyone's contribution is needed to complete the roadmap, improve data quality and raise awareness:

- **Developers and technicians** — contribute to the code and the pipeline on [GitHub](https://github.com/mxmap-it/osservatorio-nazionale-sovranita-digitale) (and on [MxMap](https://github.com/mxmap-it/mxmap.it)).
- **Researchers, journalists, activists, PA** — use the open data, report errors, propose analyses.
- **Everyone** — join the community on the [Telegram channel](https://t.me/+Ot-M_g0dkh1kMGI0) to follow updates and coordinate.

Find out how to contribute according to your profile in the [Get Involved](/en/partecipa/) section.
