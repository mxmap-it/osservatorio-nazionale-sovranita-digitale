## Summary

This inaugural report analyses the state of digital sovereignty in the Italian Public Administration, focusing on dependence on foreign email providers.

## Key findings

The analysis is based on data collected by [MxMap.it](https://mxmap.it/) on the email domains of the Italian PA registered in the Index of Public Administrations (IndicePA).

### Sovereignty of email providers

The analysis classifies each body according to the **legal sovereignty** of the provider that manages its email — that is, under which jurisdiction (Italian, European or non-EU) the communications data falls. The data reveals a significant dependence on providers subject to non-EU jurisdiction.

## Methodology

Starting from the DNS records of the Italian PA domains, for each body the email provider and the **jurisdiction** to which it is subject are identified. The report measures the legal sovereignty of the services. For more details, see the [Methodology](/en/metodologia/) section.

## Conclusions

The report highlights the need for continuous monitoring and active policies to promote the adoption of email solutions under Italian and European jurisdiction.
